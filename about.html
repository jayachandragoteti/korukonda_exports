About us

WELCOME TO KORUKONDA EXIM

KORUKONDA EXIM was established in 2011, in Tuni, Andhra Pradesh. The company is efficiently managed by the Director, Mr.Rambabu Korukonda. Under his able guidance, we have established fruitful and long term relationships with the customers. In the period of 9 years, we have developed a vast clientele in India and all over the world. Moreover, we also lay special emphasis upon the timely delivery of the consignments. This way, we make sure that we achieve the utmost satisfaction of the clients, which further helps us in building strong relations with them.

We are the chief Exporter of the Fresh Vegetables and Fresh Fruits located in Tuni, Andhra Pradesh.

We majorly deal in Vegetables like Fresh Red Onions, Fresh Yam, Fresh Chilli, Fresh Coconut, Fresh Drumstick, Fresh Bitter gourd, Fresh Lemon, Fresh Potato, Fresh Tomato, Fresh Garlic, Fresh Ginger, Fresh Pumpkin, and Fresh Capsicum etc.

We majorly deal in Fruits like Fresh Banana, Fresh Mangoes, Fresh pomegranate, Fresh Grapes, Fresh Papaya etc.

We majorly deal in Nuts and Grains like Cashew Nuts, Basmati Rice and Non Basmati rice etc.

We majorly deal in Agarbatti products like Raw Agarbatti, Bamboo Sticks, Joss Powder, Charcoal Powder etc.

We offer great value with our products and services. Introducing innovative ideas in sourcing and exporting, we have managed to improve speed, efficiency and on time delivery. All our operations are supported by a talented, hardworking human resource. With a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction, we have been offering world class products at the most competitive prices.

Why Us?

Clients are the company’s primary objective and its professionals endeavour to attain optimum level of customer satisfaction by offering the quality Products to them. Owing to our qualitative Products and other services, the company has created a wide clientele across the Globe. Moreover, Our Products can be availed at most competitive prices from us.

Quality Management

Our fruits and vegetables are checked by the quality inspector in compliance with India's strict export standards before supply. With our vast experience of regional and seasonal climatic variations, we are well qualified to select top quality fruits and vegetables from each area in accordance with the international requirements. The agricultural and market specialists working with us insist upon the optimal picking time and packing requirements of our client’s countries

Clientele

We have developed a large network of clientele which is spread all across the globe. Some of the major countries where we export are Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, U.A.E, Vietnam, London etc. Our customers are fully satisfied by our products and services which represents our high quality standards and Reliability.
